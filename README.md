
## Executive Overview

The **UIDAI Decision Intelligence System (UDIS)** is an **administrative-grade governance platform** developed for the **UIDAI Data Hackathon 2026**.

It is designed to bridge the gap between **massive Aadhaar operational datasets** and **actionable regional governance decisions**.

### What UDIS Delivers

- Converts friction telemetry into:
  - **Composite Risk Score (CRS)**
  - **Cost of Inaction (COI)**
  - **Predictive intervention planning**
- Enables:
  - Early detection of operational bottlenecks  
  - Region-specific capacity balancing  
  - Audit-grade policy advisories  

> **UDIS is a decision-support assistant.  
> It does not automate policy actions.  
> It augments human judgment.**

### 3. Explainable Rule Trace (ERT)
To meet the transparency standards of government audits (and RTI disclosures), every decision signal generated by UDIS includes an **Explainable Rule Trace**. We avoid "Black Box ML," providing clear, auditable logic for why a specific regional alert was triggered.

## UIDAI Dataset Alignment
"The Composite Risk Score (CRS) is synthesized from key operational fields in the anonymized UIDAI 2026 enrollment and update telemetry dataset, including:

• update_failures → CRS volatility component  
• biometric_latency → CRS delay component  
• enrollment_pendency → CRS pendency component  
• operator_capacity → CRS capacity component  
• district_code → Regional risk clustering  
• update_type → COI risk weighting  

All demo values are statistically synthesized to preserve UIDAI telemetry distributions without exposing real citizen data."

## Cost of Inaction (COI) Validation
"The Cost of Inaction (COI) model is validated using a 20% hold-out subset of the provided anonymized UIDAI telemetry data to ensure projection stability across seasonal and regional variance. Forecast error margins are displayed to prevent over-confidence in advisory projections."

## Human-in-the-Loop Enforcement
"All recommendations generated by UDIS require mandatory manual approval by a verified UIDAI Principal before execution. No policy action can be auto-triggered by this system under any circumstances."
